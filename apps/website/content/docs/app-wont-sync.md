If a device stops syncing, it's almost always one of a few things. Work through these in order.

## Check the basics

1. Confirm the device is **online** — sync pauses without a connection.
2. Open **Settings → Sync** and check you're **signed in**. If your session expired, sign in again.
3. Make sure every device is on a **recent version** of Companion.

## Force a resync

Still stuck? In **Settings → Sync**, choose **Resync now**. This re-checks every change against the server and resolves anything that drifted.

> **Tip:** Self-hosting? Confirm your [server](/docs/self-hosting) is reachable at the URL in your sync settings.

## Next steps

If it still won't budge, [contact us](/contact) with your device and app version.
