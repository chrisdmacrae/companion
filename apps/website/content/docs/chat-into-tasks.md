Any conversation can become a to-do list. When you've talked something through with Companion, ask it to turn the plan into tasks.

## From words to tasks

Say "make these into tasks" and the AI extracts the actionable steps, sets sensible due dates, and links each one back to the chat. Review them, tweak anything, and they're on your board.

## Keep the thread

Every generated task remembers where it came from, so months later you can still open the conversation that started it. Nothing loses its context.
