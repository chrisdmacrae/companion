Companion has a chat built in. Describe what's on your mind in plain language, and it turns your ideas into tasks, notes, and habits — right where they belong.

## A natural conversation

Ask it to "plan the week around Thursday's launch," and Companion drafts the plan, creates the tasks, and blocks focus time. Everything it makes is real, editable, and linked back to the conversation.

## It knows your workspace

Because the AI can see your notes and tasks (never your encrypted cloud data on our servers), its suggestions fit your actual projects — not generic advice.

> **Tip:** Bring your own model in **Settings → AI** if you'd rather run against your own key.
