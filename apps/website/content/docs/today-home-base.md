**Today** is where Companion opens. It gathers the tasks due now, the habits you're building, and a daily note to think in — one calm screen for the day ahead.

## Today, Upcoming, Anytime

Work is organized by when it matters, not by which list it lives in:

- **Today** — everything due now, plus your habits for the day.
- **Upcoming** — what's coming, grouped by day so nothing sneaks up.
- **Anytime** — tasks with no date, ready when you are.

## The daily note

Each day gets a fresh note, keyed to its date. Jot down what's on your mind, and turn any line into a task with `⌘ ⏎`. Yesterday's note is always a scroll away.
