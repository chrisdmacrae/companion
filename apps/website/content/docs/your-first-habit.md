Habits are the things you want to do again and again. Companion tracks them gently, celebrates your streaks, and never nags.

## Create a habit

Open **Habits** and add one — "Read 20 pages," "Move 30 min," "Meditate." Choose how often it should repeat: every day, on weekdays, or a few times a week.

## Keep the streak

Each day you complete a habit, its streak grows. The weekly ring shows how you're doing at a glance, so you can see momentum building without opening a spreadsheet.

Miss a day? Nothing breaks — your streak simply resets, and you start building again.
