Tasks are how you turn intentions into done. They can stand on their own, live inside a note, or be created for you by the AI from a conversation.

## Create a task

Press `T` anywhere, or type a line in any note and press `⌘ ⏎` to promote it to a task. Give it a due date by typing a natural phrase like "tomorrow" or "next Friday" — Companion parses it for you.

## Organize your day

Every task lands in **Today**, **Upcoming**, or **Anytime** based on its date. Check items off as you go; anything you don't finish rolls forward so nothing slips.

> **Tip:** Tasks created from a note keep a link back to it, so the context is always one click away.
